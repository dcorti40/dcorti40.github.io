About Me

Hi, I’m Devin Corti! I’m a Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a passion for designing, building, and solving real-world problems through innovative engineering solutions. Whether it’s crafting precise CAD models, machining components, or programming control systems, I enjoy turning ideas into functional, reliable designs.

My experience spans robotics, electric vehicle chassis design, and competitive engineering projects, including leading a robotics team to the FIRST World Championships and contributing to the University of Waterloo’s Formula Electric and Battle Bot teams.
Outside of engineering, I’m an avid hockey and baseball enthusiast, a volunteer coach, and a big fan of Formula 1, travel, and a good game of chess.

If you’re curious about engineering projects, technology, or just want to talk sports, feel free to connect—I’m always up for a great conversation!
